Second Floor Deluxe Room

Second floor deluxe rooms have a panoramic view of the ocean, and a high vantage point of the beach below.

Each room has a floating bed, with remote control under-bed soft illumination.

Standard occupancy for these rooms is two adults. Two more adults or children can also stay in the room on an air mattress for a small extra fee.

Bohol South Beach Hotel, Danao Beach, Panglao, Bohol, Philippines.
