Bohol South Beach Hotel, Alona Beach, Panglao, Bohol

Four Star Luxury and a warm welcome await you at our beachside location at Bohol South Beach Hotel...

This Luxury Hotel is set on the beautiful Alona Beach on the South Coast of Panglao, minutes by boat from superb diving locations.

Whether you want to dive on one of the local world-famous reefs or World War II wrecks, or lie in the warm sun, or simply chill in the shade on the rooftop, sipping a cocktail and listening to the waves gently lapping the shore below, this hotel is for you.  

Swim in the warm waters off Alona Beach, or sunbathe by our Infinity Pool. Gaze in wonder at the spectacular sunsets, ... relax as you've never relaxed before.

Dine in comfort and serenity with us, and party the night away at the nearby strip. Collect yourself at dawn and watch the splendrous sunrise from our rooftop deck

Bohol South Beach is a byword for luxury, taste and peerless service; its built a reputation for fun, modern elegance and high standards in customer care.

Come stay with us and experience tropical luxury.
