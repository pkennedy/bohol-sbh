    Bohol South Beach Hotel Location

    The hotel is located on Alona Beach, on the south coast of Panglao. It is are 16km from the Totolan causeway, approximately a 25 minute drive by Bohol South Beach Hotel shuttle van from Tagbilaran Airport on the island of Bohol.
